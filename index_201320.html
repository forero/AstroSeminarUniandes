Welcome

In this webpage we will keep track of the topics discussed (or to be discussed) during AstroLunch @Uniandes. We will meet up in Room Ip-101 every Friday at 1PM. During the first 15 minutes we will discuss the latest in the journals (Nature or Science) or any other interesting paper on the arXiv. In the next 40 minutes we will try to have 2 talks. One by somebody external to Uniandes and the second one by local students or faculty. The talks will be done on the blackboard, if you really need it you can use a couple of slides.

Ah! And this is Astro Lunch. Remember to bring your own lunch and some food to share.

Grading

If you are getting credits from this seminar, you have to be graded. We will grade attendance (20%), class participation -give at least one talk- (20%) and a project (30% first report + 30% final report). The project can be anything from studying an interesting paper to dealing with a research project that might end up being the subject for your undergrad thesis.
