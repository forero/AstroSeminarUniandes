Welcome

In this webpage we will keep track of the topics discussed (or to be discussed) during AstroLunch @Uniandes. We will meet up in Room Ip-105 every Friday at 1PM. During the first 15 minutes we will discuss the latest on Nature/Science or any other interesting paper on the arXiv. In the next 40 minutes we will try to have 2 talks. One by somebody external to Uniandes and the second one by local students or faculty. The talks will be done on the blackboard, if you really need it you can use a couple of slides.

Remember this is Astro Lunch. Remember to bring your own lunch. We will have some pizza.

Rules

If you are getting credits from this seminar, you have to be grade.d We will take attendance (20%), class participation -ask questions!- (30%) and a project (20% oral presentation + 30% written report). The project can be anything from studying an interesting paper to dealing with a research project that might end up being the theme for your undergrad thesis.

Jaime E. Forero-Romero (http://wwwprof.uniandes.edu.co/~je.forero/ email: je.forero) will lead the seminar during 2013-I. Contact him if you want to give a talk or know more about AstroLunch.
